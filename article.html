Chicago Bulls vs Indiana Pacers

Written By Jason King March 12, 2019
Sports

The battle against the Indiana Pacers on the road for the Chicago Bulls didn’t go as planned in the fourth quarter. A solid effort through the middle 24 minutes for the Chicago Bulls didn’t help them out as the team ultimately fell short by the final score of 105-96.

With the loss, the Chicago Bulls move to a record of 18-47 on the season. Any shot of doing something with this 2018-19 campaign is well in the rear view mirror for the Chicago Bulls. There is still some hope that the coaching staff can find stability and the front office figures out some answers in terms of the direction of this franchise for the remainder of this regular season.
